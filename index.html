-About-

"We shape our buildings ; thereafter they shape us"-Winston Churchill

Architecture, to me, is not just about making the things we live in, but understanding why we live in the places we build upon. I am currently studying architecture under the College of Fine Arts at Carnegie Mellon University. I look to create buildings with an emphasis on sustainable design and how to better create a symbiolic relationship between man and nature. I am also a musician,artist and composer. I have been professionally trained in classical piano for 15 years and play the violin,viola and cello as well.
